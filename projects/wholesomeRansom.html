Wholesome Ransom: GMTK Gamejam 2023

Overview

Wholesome Ransom begins with a simple mistake: you click the wrong download button out of 30 identical ones on a suspicious website.

???: "Famous hacker, how dare you get hacked!?!"

???: "Well… it's too late now. Go YEET those pesky viruses out of your Doors OS computer! And seriously—install Doors Defender next time."

This frantic mini-chaos game puts you inside a pixel-art PC simulator where pop-up ads multiply nonstop while you navigate your fake file system to locate and delete viruses before they spread.

My Role

As the Art Director, I was responsible for all visual elements, including:

  • Complete visual direction and art style
  • Creation of every in-game advertisement
  • All UI assets and in-game visuals
  • Illustrations for the intro video sequence
Aseprite Procreate Clip Studio Paint Photoshop Unity C#

Game Jam Success

Created in just 48 hours for the 2023 GMTK Game Jam under the theme "Roles Reversed." Out of 6,702 submissions, Wholesome Ransom placed in the top 5%.
