Sushi to Go: GMTK Gamejam 2022

Overview

Sushi to Go is a platformer where you create your own platforms to help a tiny sushi roll travel as far as possible on his journey to find the person who crafted him to life.

You play as Maki, a small but determined sushi roll trying to reunite with the chef who created him. Along the way, you must dodge attacks from diners’ chopsticks and chefs’ knives, while also avoiding falling onto the dirty floor—nothing ruins fresh sushi faster than dust.

A mischievous “dice of god” constantly influences your fate. It may grant you new platforms to progress or shield yourself… but it can also destroy your existing platforms, sending you tumbling to the ground. Every run becomes a test of adaptability—and luck.

My Role

This was my first-ever game jam, created in 48 hours with one other teammate for the GMTK 2022 theme "Roll of the Dice."

I contributed:

  • All visual artwork
  • All character and environment animations
  • Overall visual style and aesthetic direction

Game Jam Success

Out of 6,018 entries, Sushi to Go placed 156th, ranking in the top 2.6% of the 2022 GMTK Game Jam.

Key Features

  • Create-your-own-platform gameplay
  • Physics-based challenges driven by random dice events
  • Hand-drawn artwork and character animation
  • Charming narrative about a sushi roll seeking his creator
