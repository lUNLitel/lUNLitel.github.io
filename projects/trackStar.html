Trackstar: GMTK Gamejam 2025

Overview

Trackstar is a 3D tower defense-clicker hybrid that reimagines the genre by putting both the tower and objective in constant motion. Players control Dave, a character determined to lose weight by running laps around a track, but mischievous food items leap onto him at every turn, sabotaging his fitness goals.

Players must strategically place three types of "doggies" along the track to intercept incoming food, or click to manually swat them away. The core innovation lies in the dynamic gameplay loop—unlike traditional tower defense games with static positions, both Dave and the threats are perpetually moving, creating unique strategic challenges as players must predict and adapt to the circular track layout.
